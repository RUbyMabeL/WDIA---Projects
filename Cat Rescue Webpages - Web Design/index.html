ABOUT US

Take Meow Home Animal Rescue is a Ottawa-based nonprofitable organization helping rescue cats get to their forever homes.

What you can do to Help?

Donation

We need donations to support us to keep saving and taking care of more cats. You can help us by donating money or daily necessicities for our cats such as cat litters, cat porches, cat toys, etc. We promise all the donations will go directly to saving and taking care of cats. Donations cover costs related to transportation, fostering, and rescuing cats from a variety of situations such as neglect, and abuse. Donate now to help save a life!

Volunteer

We are in need of volunteers who love and have patience with cats, and/or can help us with a variety of tasks that is about animal rescue and keeping our organization running! If you want to help us as well as spend time playing and taking care of cats, please click here to submit your volunteer application. We thank you so much for your kindness and efforts!

Adopt One or More!

If you are considering adopting a cute cat, you can visit us! We have many cute cats as well as kittens in our shelter - please submit an adoption application if you are interested in adopting one or more of them! The cats on the list of waiting for adoption are all well-behaved babies. Please click here to apply! We will go through your application and then contact you as soon as possible!
